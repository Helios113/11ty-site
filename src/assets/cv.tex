\section*{Abstract}
My research focuses on creating new foundational model architectures which are more parameter and data efficient, thus they require much less compute for training and inference
The reduced parameter size leads to a higher throughput and lower latency and enables more capable edge model deployments.
I specifically work on dynamic iterative transformer models which allow test time compute scaling and circuit composition, as I believe that future of foundational models, similar to biological brains, will have a dynamic compute path.
